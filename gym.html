Weight Training

Weight Training is the use of resistance to muscular contraction to build strength, anaerobic endurance and size of skeletal muscles.

General Fitness

It is one's ability to execute daily activities with optimal performance, endurance, and strength with the management of disease, fatigue, and stress and reduced sedentary behavior

Cardio Training

It is any form of exercise that elevates the heart rate due to the repeated contraction of muscles while utilizing the aerobic and even, at times, the anaerobic energy system.
